Overview

Guesser Space is a brain-training game app. We are committed to collecting as little personal data as possible. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how it is handled.

Data We Collect

2.1 Stored only on your device

The following information never leaves your device and is stored in local app storage (cleared when you uninstall):

DataPurpose
Optional player nicknamePersonalise milestone messages. Never required.
Onboarding preferencesPersonalise the app experience.
Language preferenceDisplay the app in your chosen language.
First launch dateCalculate free trial days remaining.
Trial and referral bonus daysTrack your earned trial period.
Referral code flagsPrevent duplicate referral rewards.
Subscription promo flagRecord whether a promo code was applied.

2.2 Sent to Firebase (Google)

We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to generate a stable, anonymous device identifier (UID). This UID is not linked to your name, email, or any personal identity — it is used solely to power the referral system.

We use Cloud Firestore to store referral codes. Each document contains only the anonymous UID of the code owner and a list of anonymous UIDs of devices that redeemed it. No names, emails, or personally identifiable information are ever stored.

Firebase is operated by Google LLC. Google's privacy policy →

2.3 Sent to RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. On purchase or restore, RevenueCat receives your app store receipt and a device identifier. It does not receive your name, email, or payment card details. RevenueCat's privacy policy →

2.4 Handled by app stores

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple or Google. We do not receive or store payment information.

  • Google Privacy Policy

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Precise or approximate location
  • Camera, microphone, or contacts access
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA, GAID)
  • Browsing history or data from other apps
  • Any data from children beyond what is described above

Data Retention

  • On-device data — retained until you uninstall the app.
  • Firebase Anonymous UID — persists until you request deletion or Firebase cleans up stale accounts (typically after 30 days of inactivity).
  • Firestore referral records — retained while the referral programme is active. Request deletion anytime at mdieska1@gmail.com.
  • RevenueCat records — subject to RevenueCat's own retention policy.

Children's Privacy

Guesser Space does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your country). The app requires no account creation and displays no advertising. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us at mdieska1@gmail.com.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA, UK, or another jurisdiction with data protection laws, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, object to, or receive a portable copy of your data. Because most data is stored only on your device, uninstalling the app is the most effective way to exercise these rights. For Firebase or Firestore data, contact us and we will respond within 30 days.

Security

We use Firebase's encryption in transit (TLS) and server-side encryption at rest. The anonymous auth credential is secured by the platform keychain (iOS) or keystore (Android). No sensitive personal data is stored in plain text.
