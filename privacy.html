1. Overview

Guesser Space is a brain-training game application developed by Apakhian Games. We are committed to collecting as little personal data as necessary to operate the application. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is handled. Most data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

2. Data We Collect

2.2 Firebase Authentication (Google LLC)

We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to assign each new installation a stable, anonymous device identifier (UID). This UID is not linked to any personal identity and is used solely to power the referral system and leaderboard.

Users may optionally link their Google account to back up and restore progress across devices. If you choose to link a Google account, Firebase Authentication receives your Google email address, display name, and profile picture URL from Google. This information is used only to authenticate your account and associate your leaderboard data with your Google identity. It is not shared with third parties beyond the Google and Firebase infrastructure described in this policy.

Firebase is operated by Google LLC. Google Privacy Policy →

2.3 Cloud Firestore (Google LLC)

We use Cloud Firestore to store the following data on our servers:

CollectionData storedPurpose
rankings/{uid} Firebase UID, optional player name, total XP, per-game high scores, timestamp Global leaderboard and cross-device progress backup/restore
usernames/{username} Chosen display name, linked Firebase UID, timestamp Enforce unique display names on the leaderboard
referrals/{code} Code owner UID, list of redeeming UIDs, redemption count Referral system — enforce limits and award bonus trial days

All UIDs stored are Firebase-issued identifiers. If you have not linked a Google account, your UID is fully anonymous. Leaderboard display names are optional and always chosen by you.

2.4 RevenueCat, Inc.

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. On purchase or restore, RevenueCat receives your app store receipt and a Firebase identifier. RevenueCat does not receive your name, email address, or payment card details. Subscription status is used solely to unlock Pro features within the application. RevenueCat Privacy Policy →

2.5 Payment processors — Apple App Store and Google Play

All payments are processed exclusively by Apple Inc. or Google LLC through their respective app stores. We do not receive or store payment card information.

5. Data Retention

  • On-device data — retained until you uninstall the application.
  • Firebase Anonymous UID — persists until you request deletion or Firebase automatically removes stale anonymous accounts (typically 30 days of inactivity for anonymous-only accounts).
  • Firestore rankings and usernames — retained while your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.
  • Firestore referral records — retained while the referral programme is active. Request deletion at mdieska1@gmail.com.
  • RevenueCat records — subject to RevenueCat's own retention policy.

6. Children's Privacy

Guesser Space does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). The application requires no account creation and displays no advertising. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us at mdieska1@gmail.com and we will take prompt steps to delete it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including the European Economic Area and the United Kingdom under the GDPR), you may have the right to access, rectify, erase, object to processing of, or receive a portable copy of your personal data. Because most data is stored only on your device, uninstalling the application is the most direct means of exercising deletion rights for on-device data. For data held in Firestore, contact us at mdieska1@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

All data transmitted to Firebase and RevenueCat is encrypted in transit using TLS. Firebase applies server-side encryption at rest for all stored data. The Firebase authentication credential is secured by the platform keychain (iOS) or keystore (Android). No sensitive personal data is stored in plain text by the application.
